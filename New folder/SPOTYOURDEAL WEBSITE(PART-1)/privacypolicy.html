Partnership & Earning

Grow With SPOT & Grow Your Business - Become SPOT Partner

Become SPOT Franchise Partner - SPOT Franchise Partner Program is designed by keeping in mind all kinds of entrepreneurs and startups in mind. SPOT has a place for everyone such as Retailer (B2C), Startup Business Selling Any Services or Products (B2C), Wholesalers & Manufacturers (B2B) etc. Our model eliminates the need for you to have a physical office or retail store, spend for local advertising, or hire many on-the-ground personnel. You can single handedly be a channel franchise partner and earn on sales of other businesses or sell your products or just decide to deliver

Earn On Sales - SPOT Franchise can help bringing more retailers from his or her territory and earn commission on their sales. Alternatively franchise can list retailers and have them listed products under his franchise store to sell managing orders through franchise but product listing from retailers. See below partnership Types

Earn On Retailer & Wholesaler Onboarding - SPOT Franchise can earn per retailer and wholesaler registration and onboarding at SPOT.

Earn On Customer Onboarding & Downloads - SPOT Franchise can earn per customer registration and onboarding at SPOT after successful download of their mobile app.

Types Of Franchise Partnership

Franchise Only - If an individual or a business party wants to work as a commission agent for the retailers and businesses from his or her territory to earn commission on the sales from the businesses.

Delivery Franchise - If the franchise partner wants to set up delivery business also in addition to growing and promoting retailers and business in his or her territory.

Delivery Franchise & Seller - If the franchise partner wants to deliver goods and services to the consumers but also wants to sell directly to consumers then can direct orders from the consumers as B2C. .

Types Of Retail & Wholesale Business Partnerships

Retailer & Seller - If a business wants to list and sell products themselves at the portal for direct to consumer selling (B2C).

Supplier Only - If a business wants to supply its products to franchise to sell but want to list and display at a portal then they can register as suppliers only. In this case the supplier will act as a B2B seller for the franchise seller. Difference is that supplier may do it's own B2C promotion also although orders will be managed by the franchise.

Delivery Franchise & Seller - If the franchise partner wants to deliver goods and services to the consumers but also wants to sell directly to consumers then can direct orders from the consumers as B2C.
